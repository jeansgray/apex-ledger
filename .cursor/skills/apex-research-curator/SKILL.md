# Apex Research Curator

## Focus

Personal investor scope:

- Top holdings and sector concentration
- Question-specific macro drivers (rates, earnings, policy)
- Recent issuer events if symbol known

## Procedure

1. Anchor on symbols from the ledger snapshot.
2. Tie research bullets to the user's question — avoid encyclopedic dumps.
3. Separate **facts** from **interpretation**.

## Output

Bullet list (5–8 items):

- `[fact]` ...
- `[interpretation]` ...

Do not recommend specific trades in this skill.
